REDLining Manager//
  • Do your document redlining processes hinder business productivity?
  • Do the external and internal reviews of these redlinings lead to inaccurate document versions across your teams?
  • Is your review process manual? Resulting in lack of data to make internal processes more efficient?

If you answer yes to any of these questions, REDLining Manager (RLM) is your solution.

REDLining Manager by Kemisoft resolves these painful business process issues so you can focus on the heart of your business.

  • Automation of your document creation process; reduces out dated documents being used; reduces the opportunity for errors by populating templated documents with key information pulled from CRM
  • Externally presenting the documents in the most common formats, including,(MS WORD, PDF, etc)
  • Audit tracking of all edits maintained in CRM
  • Writeback edits can be done to both the document and CRM records (ex: legal name of the company is edited, it will update the CRM record if desired)
  • Version and Approval/Rejection tracking
  • Analytics tracking (ex: who is reviewing which document, how long has it been in their queue to review)

REDLining Manager (RLM) is purpose built for Salesforce and Nintex solutions.
